Apply for the Fulton County Homestead Exemption

Fulton County homeowners are entitled to a homestead tax exemption on their home property provided the home was owned by the homeowner and was their legal residence as of January 1.

In order to complete this application, you will need the following:

  • The parcel number for your property.
  • A picture or scanned copy of state-issued identification or driver's license.
  • A picture or scanned copy of vehicle registration for each vehicle registered.

This form takes 10 minutes to fill out.

Parcel Number

There are three ways to find your parcel number.

  • Use Fulton County's official search tool.
  • It's on your Annual Notice of Assessment.
  • It's on your property tax bill.
